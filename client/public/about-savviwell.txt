
About SavviWell

SavviWell is a voice-activated AI lifestyle assistant designed to reduce the mental load of meal planning and support everyday wellness. We help individuals and families eat healthier, feel better, and stay organized—without the stress.

With personalized meal plans, smart grocery lists, allergy-aware swaps, and wellness nudges, SavviWell adapts to real-time needs like dietary preferences, health goals, and pantry inventory. Whether you're cooking from scratch or need a quick backup plan, SavviWell offers intuitive support to make meals manageable and health accessible.

We're here for busy caregivers, wellness seekers, and anyone who wants to use food as a tool for better living. Our mission is simple: to make daily nourishment easier, more joyful, and more sustainable.
