Core Innovation — The Family Fork:
SavviWell's proprietary branching engine takes a single base meal concept and automatically generates personalized variants for every member of a household. It maintains household cohesion (everyone eats "the same thing") while respecting individual dietary needs, allergies, preferences, and age-appropriate portions. Ingredient lists are automatically merged and deduplicated across all branches to produce a single optimized grocery list.

Key Features:
- Family Fork — multi-diet branching engine for household meal planning
- Multi-diet meal planning with per-member dietary profiles
- Automated Grocery Integration — consolidated, store-optimized shopping lists
- Voice-first AI interaction for hands-free kitchen use
- Adaptive meal recommendations that learn from household feedback
- Allergy-aware recipe swaps with safety guardrails
- Batch cooking and meal prep optimization
- Smart pantry awareness to reduce food waste
- Household-centric data model (not individual-centric)

Core Mission:
To eliminate household food-decision fatigue by providing a Kitchen Operating System that automates the cognitive labor behind multi-diet meal planning, grocery management, and family nutrition — replacing fragmented tools with unified decision infrastructure.

Use Cases:
- A family of four where one parent is heart-healthy, a child has a nut allergy, and a toddler needs age-appropriate portions — all eating the same base meal
- A busy parent needing a weekly meal plan that respects multiple dietary constraints and produces one consolidated grocery list
- A caregiver supporting an aging parent's dietary needs alongside the rest of the household
- A wellness-conscious adult tracking personalized nutrition goals with voice-first interaction

Anti-Inflammatory Case Study — Vegetarian Lentil Chili:
SavviWell treats a Lentil & Kidney Bean Chili as a high-fiber, plant-based foundation and branches the service based on individual member Health Tags. Fork A (The Health Lead) serves over cauliflower rice with guacamole — blunting glucose spikes and prioritizing antioxidant-rich fats. Fork B (The Energy Lead) serves over brown rice with shredded cheddar — satisfying higher caloric needs while maintaining the same base protein. The Swap Engine autonomously replaces disliked ingredients (e.g., bell peppers for sweet potatoes), programmatically adjusting the grocery list and cooking instructions. One pot, multiple dietary outcomes, supply-chain ready.
